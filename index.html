How to play Spaceman

  1. Player 1, input a word to be guessed in the appropriate box. Be sure to submit.
  2. A blank line for each letter of the word will display across the board.
  3. Player 2, input your guesses, one letter at a time, into the appropriate box. Be sure to submit.
  4. The round is over when either the word is guessed, or the spaceship is completely visible.
  5. Just submit another word to start a new round.
  6. Good luck!

This will show the win or lose message.
